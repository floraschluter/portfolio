Who Am I?

I'm an INFJ and digital marketer who thrives with companies I'm passionate about. I'm an extroverted introvert who loves meaningful connections. I'm a leader, a doer, and a life-long learner. I often enjoy trying new things and exploring. I am often intrigued by the world around me - not only by its physicalities but more importantly by its deeper meanings and roots. In exploring the world, I become more sensitive to the world around me and therein more in-tune with it. I am compassionate, sensitive, and highly emotional. I think it is important to put emotion into anything that I do. Putting emotion into what I do equates to putting heart and my best effort into activities I dedicate my time toward. I'm strategic and tactical, a visionary who loves the details, creative, yet practical. People often describe me as: driven, passionate, and ambitious. Which pretty much means that I care.
